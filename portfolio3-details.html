Project Overview

This project was in time series analysis, and 3 presentations were given via zoom to roughly 60 statistics graduate students during each phase of the project [videos linked above]. The focus of the project was to perform analysis of daily measurements of an air pollution index, PM2.5, taken in a suburb near downtown Los Angeles, and use a formulated model to make forecasts. In the end we did this with 2 different modeling approaches, and compared the forecast performances of their respective chosen models.
