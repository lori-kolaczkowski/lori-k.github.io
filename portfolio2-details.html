Project Details

This project is for a course in statistical consulting, in which students are tasked with developing an analysis to answer a fictional client’s statistical question regarding their data. Students find and clean their data prior to analysis and the primary focus of the project report and presentation is effective communication in answering the client’s question.

The data I have chosen is a portion of the dataset studied in a paper by Silvana Debernardi and colleagues, published in December of 2020, in PLOS Medicine.

An analysis is developed as if a client had brought in this data and asked for statistical expertise regarding the analysis being undertaken to address their study goals.
